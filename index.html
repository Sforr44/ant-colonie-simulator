Game Guide

Objective: Build and manage an ant colony. Gather food, dig tunnels, spawn ants, and defend against enemies.

Controls:

  • Arrow keys or WASD: Move the main ant
  • Spacebar: Gather food
  • Mouse click on canvas: Move ant to position
  • Buttons: Dig tunnels, gather food, spawn ants

Rules:

  • Gather food to spawn more ants
  • Dig tunnels to expand your colony
  • Defend against red enemies
  • Manage resources wisely for long-term survival
